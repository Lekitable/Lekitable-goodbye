Epilogue

April 8, 2023

One and a half years ago, we set out to create an open, green network of products. We believed that people whould love to support local community and local stores that support the environment.

Along the way, we created an ecommerce platform available on the web and iOS that proved these things to be true. We’re proud of the product we built, but even more so, we’re grateful for the community of partners that made it grow.

At every turn, your feedback shaped Lekitable's development, and your visions signaled a new chapter for ecommerce logistics. Together we wrote in the margins of anti-Amazon era. Thank you for helping to bring this reality into view.

Lekitable's story ends here. Many challenges in the world of ecommerce marketplace remain unsolved, and we failed to create a sustainable platform for trading products and connect with stores that take actions for the environemnt. For this, we’re deeply sorry. We considered every option before making the difficult decision to end the product that brought us together.

As of today, it is no longer possible to create a new account, and on June 19, 2023, the Lekitable app will no longer be available. For the next three months, our first priority is to help you transition to other services and get back to reading. All of your data are deleted and they cannot be retreived.

Our team will continue to pursue their studies in their favorable majors and we are sure that they will accomplish what they have dreamed of.

Thank you for the time and attention you’ve contributed to this community. It has been a privilege to work together, and we look forward to meeting again, in new ways, in the margins.

Sincerely,
Vasilis, Jules, Isaë - Lekitable Team

FAQ

How long will my books and reading data be available?

Your data will be available until April 8, 2023.

What happens to my data? Will my privacy be protected?

All of your data and account information will be permanently deleted after April 9, 2023. User information will not be archived, shared or saved in any way.

Can I continue using the Lekitable app?

You can not. The app will not be updated or supported after April 8, 2023, and any functionality will be deactivated.

Where should I search for sustainable ecommerce now?

We think that the best approach on this would be to check out Vinted.

Help! I still have questions.

We’re still here to answer your questions. Please write us at contact@lekitable.fr, or find us on Twitter or Facebook.
